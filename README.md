# Super-Sales-Dashboard-PowerBI


# Objective-
To contribute to the succces of a business by utilizing data analysis techiques , spacifically forecasting on time series analysis , to provide valuable insight and accurate sales forecasting.

Description

The objective of this project can be broken down into the following components:

1. Dashboard Creation
Identified key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure business performance.
Designed an intuitive and visually appealing dashboard.
Added interactive visualizations and filtering capabilities.
Enabled users to explore data at different levels of granularity.
Presented sales trends, regional performance, and forecasting insights in a clear and actionable format.
2. Sales Forecasting
Applied time series analysis techniques to forecast future sales.
Generated a 15-day sales forecast to support business planning.
Analyzed historical sales patterns and trends.
3. Business Insights
Identified top-performing states and regions.
Evaluated sales performance over time.
Provided insights to support strategic business decisions.
